Essential Gear

Having the right gear makes a HUGE difference on your camping trip. Over time you'll discover what works best for you. In the meantime, here are some of my tips and recommendations for camping gear.

  • A three-season sleeping bag
  • The smallest tent that meets your needs
  • Access to 3 gallons of water a day
  • Lots of versatile layers
  • A buddy

Remember to check your gear before you strike out and always assume you'll be in extreme temperatures. Better to be over-prepared!
